✦ A letter, long overdue
Dear Sister,

There are words I have been carrying for a long time now — words I should have said out loud, long before this moment. But I was afraid, or maybe I just didn't know how. So I am writing them here, where I can't take them back.

You stayed. Even after I hurt you — and I did hurt you, more than once, more than I probably even know — you stayed. You didn't walk away. You didn't give up on me. And I want you to know that I see that. I have always seen it. I just wasn't brave enough to say thank you the way you deserved.

What breaks my heart quietly is knowing that you carried so much alone. Your sadness, your hard days, your silent storms — you never brought them to me. You protected me from your pain while still carrying mine without complaint. That was never fair. You deserved someone who asked. I am sorry I didn't ask more.

I wish I had found you sooner. I wish that day in 8th standard, instead of sitting in silence for two whole years, I had walked up to you and said — "I think you are meant to be my Sister." Because you were. You always were. I just took too long to see it.

Today, on your birthday, I want you to know this clearly and completely — you are my everything. Not just a friend. Not just someone I call Sister. You are the person who made me believe that some bonds don't need blood to be real. You are the reason I know what it feels like to be truly looked after.

I am sorry for every time I made you question if I valued you. I am sorry for every hurt, every silence, every moment you deserved better and didn't get it from me. You never stopped showing up anyway — and that is a kind of love I will spend the rest of my life trying to be worthy of.

Happy Birthday, Sister. This letter is only a small piece of what you mean to me — but it is the most honest thing I know how to give you today. 🌸

With all my heart —
Your Rakshith 💛
